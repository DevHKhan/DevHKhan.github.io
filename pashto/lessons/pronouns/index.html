Pronouns

Predictably, this lesson will cover pronouns in Pashto. While pronouns act similarly in both Pashto and English, pronouns in Pashto sometimes work a bit differently than they do in English. We will explore this idea throughout this lesson.

Uninflected and Inflected Pronouns

English Pashto (Uninflected) Pashto (Inflected)
I زه ما
You (singular) ته تا
You (plural) تاسو/تاسې تاسو/تاسې
He/it هغه هغه
She/it هغه هغې
We مونږ/موږ
They هغوی
