Verbs

It's now time to look at verbs in Pashto! These verbs will help add on to your knowledge of pronouns and Pashto as a whole.

Copula Verbs (Present Tense)

What on Earth are copula verbs? Well, they can essentially be thought of as verbs connecting subjects of sentences to other descriptive words. Verbs like "to be" in English come to mind here. We will look at some of these "to be" verbs in Pashto below.

English Pronoun Pashto Pronoun English Verb Pashto Verb
I زه Am یم
You (singular) ته Are یې
You (plural) تاسو/تاسې Are یئ
He/it هغه Is دی
She/it هغه Is ده
We مونږ/موږ Are یو
They هغوی Are دي

Copula Verbs (Past Tense)

Here, we'll look at the same verbs. The only difference is that these ones are in the past tense.

English Pronoun Pashto Pronoun English Verb Pashto Verb
I زه Was وم
You (singular) ته Were وې
You (plural) تاسو/تاسې Were وئ
He/it هغه Was و
She/it هغه Was وه
We مونږ/موږ Were وو
They (masculine) هغوی Were وو
They (feminine) هغوی Were وې
