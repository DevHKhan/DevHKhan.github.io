Verbs

It's now time to look at verbs in Pashto! These verbs will help add on to your knowledge of pronouns and Pashto as a whole.

Copula Verbs (Present Tense)

What on Earth are copula verbs? Well, they can essentially be thought of as verbs connecting subjects of sentences to other descriptive words. Verbs like "to be" in English come to mind here. We will look at some of these "to be" verbs in Pashto below.

English Pronoun Pashto Pronoun English Verb Pashto Verb
I زه - [zə] Am یم - [jəm]
You (singular) ته - [t̪ə] Are یې - [je]
You (plural) تاسو/تاسې - [ˈt̪a.so] / [ˈt̪a.se] Are یئ - [jej]
He/it هغه - [haˈɣa] Is دی - [d̪aj]
She/it هغه - [haˈɣa] Is ده - [d̪a]
We مونږ - [mung] / موږ - [mug] Are یو - [ju]
They هغوی - [haɣwi] Are دي - [d̪i]

Copula Verbs (Past Tense)

Here, we'll look at the same verbs. The only difference is that these ones are in the past tense.

English Pronoun Pashto Pronoun English Verb Pashto Verb
I زه - [zə] Was وم - [wum]
You (singular) ته - [t̪ə] Were وې - [we]
You (plural) تاسو/تاسې - [ˈt̪a.so] / [ˈt̪a.se] Were وئ - [wej]
He/it هغه - [haˈɣa] Was و - [wo]
She/it هغه - [haˈɣa] Was وه - [wa]
We مونږ - [mung] / موږ - [mug] Were وو - [wu]
They (masculine) هغوی - [haˈɣwi] Were وو - [wu]
They (feminine) هغوی - [haˈɣwi] Were وې - [we]

Copula Verbs (Future Tense)

Below are the future tense versions of the same verbs seen above. There is an important nuance to note, however: with these future tense verbs, the word به is used after the subject pronoun and before the verb.

English Pronoun Pashto Pronoun English Verb Pashto Verb
I زه (به) - [zə (ba)] Will be یم - [jəm]
You (singular) ته (به) - [t̪ə (ba)] Will be یې - [je]
You (plural) تاسو/تاسې (به) - [ˈt̪ɑ.so/ˈt̪ɑ.se (ba)] Will be یئ - [jej]
He/she/it هغه (به) - [haˈɣa (ba)] Will be وي - [wi]
We مونږ/موږ (به) - [mung/mug (ba)] Will be یو - [ju]
They هغوی (به) - [haˈɣwi (ba)] Will be وي - [wi]
